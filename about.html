Within the walls of our manufacture in La Chaux-de-Fonds, a team of skilled artisans dedicates their expertise to creating timepieces of exceptional quality. Each watchmaker brings years of experience and an unwavering attention to detail.

Our workshops are equipped with both traditional tools and cutting-edge technology, allowing us to maintain the highest standards while embracing innovation. From hand-finishing movement components to setting precious stones, every step reflects our commitment to excellence.

The creation of a single Lorent timepiece can take several months, a testament to the care and precision invested in each piece.
